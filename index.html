Xand is Nigeria's first of it's kind food magazine positioned as a nutritional and culinary manual, for discerning Nigerians, who love the exciting art of cookery, as well as the 'wining & dining' lifestyle.

XAND consulting is Nigeria's first and finest kitchen affairs magazine dedicated to getting you cooking tips, ideas, news, gists & hacks.

OUR MISSION

Our quest is to promote the lifestyle of 'well-feeding' among Nigerians by sourcing, documenting, and sharing information (by means of written, digital, and experimental media) that enable mind and body wellness for all members of the family.

OUR VISION

To become for Nigeria, the undisputed authority for first class information that enable a wealthier nation sustained by healthier families made up of stronger, smarter, and happier people living well by feeding well
